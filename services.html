Services

Our experienced team provides cost effective and efficient services

MetResponse explores, identifies and validates biomarkers and gene pathways from basic biological to clinical and drug studies. We provide four types of services:

Experimental Design

We custom-design experimental strategies for metabolomics, gene expression and proteomics from basic science to clinical studies. Complex systems require personalized design for data collection and analysis. Metresponse offers more efficient detection methods for mapping and understanding complex systems.

Data Collection

We analyze your samples for metabolomics, lipidomics, and proteomics data collection and analysis. We provide in house human expertise in addition to software for novice users which can help you find manageable solutions at affordable prices in a timely manner.

Data Analysis

We analyze your previously collected data for pathway analysis and biomarker discovery for genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics, lipidomics, and metabolomics data using biostatistics, bioinformatics analysis, and our in-house pathway analysis to generate easy-to-read reports and to recommend manageable solutions to specific data sets.

Validation & Pathway Analysis

We custom-design targeted experiments & collaborate with data collection and analysis using stable isotope labeling technologies for biomarker and pathway validation.

Collaboration:

We are experienced in grant writing and open to collaborating with academic and clinical researchers and other companies interested in metabolomics.

Not sure which service is right for you?

We are available for free consultation through Skype. If you are in Madison, WI, in-person meetings can be arranged. For more information regarding our services contact us.
